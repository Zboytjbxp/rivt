Subcontractor Agreement Reference

RIVT does not create an employment, payroll, agency, or escrow relationship between users. Contractors and tradespeople are responsible for the contracts they choose to use for each job.

Recommended Work Terms

Before work begins, both sides should agree on scope, schedule, location, pay, materials, tools, insurance expectations, change orders, safety requirements, cancellation terms, and payment timing.

Independent Responsibility

Users remain responsible for licenses, permits, insurance, tax reporting, worker classification, and compliance with federal, state, and local law.

Records

RIVT records, photos, daily logs, invoices, and messages are tools for documentation. They are not a substitute for legal advice or a written agreement reviewed by a qualified professional.
