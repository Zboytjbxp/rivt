# RIVT Product Contract

Status: Gate A working contract
Source of truth: `RIVT_MASTER_BUILD_PROMPT.md`

## Product

RIVT is the professional network and operating platform for skilled trades. It connects contractors, trade businesses, subcontractors, skilled tradespeople, and crews so they can find work or help, build trusted relationships, coordinate jobs, and preserve professional records.

RIVT is not a homeowner marketplace, staffing agency, employer of record, payroll provider, escrow service, or party to the work agreement. It does not sell homeowner leads and does not take a percentage of job payments in Gate A.

Tagline: **Where skilled trades connect.**

## Gate A Outcome

Prove that invited Jacksonville users can complete one trustworthy work loop:

1. Create and verify an account.
2. Complete a role-correct professional profile.
3. Post or discover a real job.
4. Apply or invite.
5. Exchange and accept an offer mutually.
6. Communicate in a job-linked thread.
7. Document work and completion.
8. Leave an eligible two-sided review.

The pilot succeeds when legitimate work relationships complete with repeat intent and manageable support burden. Signups alone are not success.

## Users

- **Contractor:** contractor, trade business owner, or authorized company representative. Role does not automatically mean licensed.
- **Tradesperson:** independent subcontractor, skilled trades worker, freelancer, or crew member.
- **Internal:** support, moderator, verifier, and administrator with least-privilege access.

Primary role is selected during onboarding and is not switched from the application header.

## Jacksonville Pilot

- Invitation-only.
- Jacksonville-area configuration, not hardcoded UI strings.
- Priority trade clusters require founder approval before recruitment.
- Concierge support is permitted, but every manual intervention is recorded.
- No fake jobs, fake people, fake reviews, fake activity, or hidden production seed data.

## Trust Language

- Self-reported, uploaded, reviewed, verified, expired, rejected, and unavailable are different states.
- RIVT does not promise that a user is qualified for a particular job.
- RIVT must not claim users can simply work under another contractor's license.
- Exact addresses remain private until the approved relationship stage.
- RIVT records direct-payment information but does not process or protect job payments in Gate A.

## Navigation

Mobile destinations: Home, Work, Crew, Shop Talk, Tools.

Messages, notifications, search, and profile are top-bar actions. Shop Talk and Tools may remain feature-flagged during Gate A, but the pilot must not expose dead destinations. Do not use a More drawer or Contractor/Tradesperson toggle.

## Gate A Non-Goals

- Homeowner accounts or homeowner acquisition.
- Facebook or Apple OAuth.
- On-platform job payments, escrow, payroll, tax filing, or 1099 issuance.
- Subscription charging.
- Public Shop Talk, Trade News, advanced tools, advanced crew management, AI matching, background checks, or nationwide launch.
- Automated licensing, insurance, legal, safety, or compliance decisions.

## Success Measures

- Time to verified account and completed profile.
- Jobs posted by legitimate pilot contractors.
- Qualified applications/invites per job.
- Time to first qualified response.
- Mutual offer acceptance rate.
- Active work completion rate.
- Project-record and completion-proof adoption.
- Eligible review completion.
- Repeat-collaboration intent or action.
- Support interventions, reports, disputes, no-shows, and safety incidents.
