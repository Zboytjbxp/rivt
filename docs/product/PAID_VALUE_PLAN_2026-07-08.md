# RIVT Paid Value Plan — July 8, 2026

## Research signal

Competitors do not charge only for "finding work." They charge for making the job easier to run after the first conversation:

- CompanyCam positions paid value around jobsite photo/video documentation, organized project history, reports, checklists, offline sync, and keeping field/office aligned.
- Jobber's Client Hub concentrates quotes, approvals, appointment details, invoices, receipts, and follow-up work in one mobile-friendly place.
- Housecall Pro sells field-service workflow: estimating, scheduling, invoicing, customer communication, reporting, and job details.
- Joist focuses on contractor estimates, invoices, payments, receipts, templates, and project organization from a phone.

## Product position

RIVT should not ask users to pay for thin marketplace access during Jacksonville launch. The paid wedge should be:

> Pro keeps the proof, paperwork, and closeout trail for every accepted job.

That fits trades-only RIVT because the same person often has to prove what happened on site, protect payment, send paperwork, and keep repeat-work history.

## What makes $20–$30/month plausible later

1. Job proof packets
   - Accepted job workspace with photos, files, notes, completion status, and closeout report.
   - Clear "ready for closeout" state.
   - Easy export/share path later.

2. Better paperwork
   - Estimate to invoice.
   - Invoice from active job.
   - Receipts/payment records without taking a cut of the job.

3. Contractor memory
   - Crew bench, past jobs, photos, reviews, response history, and notes.
   - Less retyping across estimates, invoices, records, and messages.

4. Trust without false claims
   - Evidence states only: uploaded certificate, self-reported insurance, submitted proof, confirmed closeout.
   - No fake verified labels.

## Current build decision

Do not raise price in code yet. Keep launch price intact. First make the product visibly worth paying for:

- tighten Pro copy around records, closeout packets, and paperwork;
- add a "Job proof packet" module inside Records;
- avoid adding fake value claims or anything that depends on unbuilt exports.

## Later paid-tier test

When Jacksonville has enough active usage, test:

- Free: browse, apply/post limited work, Shop Talk, basic calculator, basic invoice draft.
- Pro at $19–$29/month: job proof packets, closeout exports, longer record history, advanced invoice/estimate exports, rate/templates, contractor bench history.

Do not gate tradespeople from applying to work.
