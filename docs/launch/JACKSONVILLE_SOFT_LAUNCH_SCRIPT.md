# RIVT Jacksonville Soft Launch Script

Audience: licensed contractors, subcontractors, independent skilled tradespeople, and small crews in the Jacksonville area.

Boundary: RIVT is trades-only. Do not position it as a homeowner lead marketplace.

## Founder DM / Text

Hey, I am opening a small Jacksonville pilot for RIVT, a trades-only network for contractors and skilled subs.

It is built for finding help, finding side work, asking trade questions, sharing job photos, keeping records, and building a reputation with other people in the trades. No homeowner leads, no Angi-style bidding, and no transaction cut on the work.

I am keeping the first group small so I can fix anything fast. Would you be open to trying it and giving honest feedback?

Site: https://rivt.pro

## In-Person Supply House Pitch

I am building RIVT here in Jacksonville. It is a trades-only app for contractors and subs to find each other, post work, ask field questions, and build a real profile around their work.

The first pilot is small and local. I am not trying to sell homeowner leads. I want contractors and tradespeople who will actually use it and tell me what is missing before we open it wider.

## First-Week Operating Plan

Day 1:
- Invite 10 contractors and 10 tradespeople from separate networks.
- Ask every contractor to post one real or upcoming work need.
- Ask every tradesperson to complete profile basics and answer or ask one Shop Talk question.

Day 2:
- Message every new user personally.
- Ask what felt confusing, slow, or missing.
- Fix obvious copy, layout, and dead-click issues before adding more users.

Day 3:
- Invite 15 more users only if support inbox, monitoring, and smoke tests are healthy.
- Track jobs posted, applications, messages, Shop Talk posts, and support requests.

Days 4-7:
- Keep support response under one business day.
- Do not change pricing mid-pilot.
- Do not launch broad ads until the first cohort can post, apply, message, and use Tools without founder hand-holding.

## Success Signals

- Contractors post real work without a walkthrough.
- Tradespeople understand Work, Crew, Shop Talk, Tools, and Profile on mobile.
- At least one contractor-to-sub conversation starts inside RIVT.
- Shop Talk gets real questions or answers from the first cohort.
- Users can explain RIVT as a trades-only network in their own words.

## Stop Conditions

- Login/signup breaks for more than one user.
- Job posting, applying, messaging, or billing fails in production.
- Support inbox or monitoring is not being watched.
- Users repeatedly think RIVT is for homeowners.
- A mobile screen has hidden buttons, horizontal scrolling, or blocked actions.
