# Packet 25 - Tool Consolidation

## Goal

Make Tools feel like a focused field kit instead of a catalog of overlapping
mini-apps. Keep frequent field work obvious, place supporting work in short
purpose-based groups, and remove unfinished or duplicative utilities from the
launch surface without deleting underlying user records.

## Scope

- Keep the five core field apps visible: Heavy 16th, Estimate, Invoice, Daily
  log, and Camera.
- Keep Materials, Price book, Time, Expenses, Mileage, Receivables, Punch
  list, Safety, and Tax summary reachable in three compact groups.
- Remove duplicate, unfinished, or legally sensitive launchers from the hub:
  Bid builder, Earnings, Tax estimator, Daily report, Job checklists, and
  Contracts.
- Remove decorative per-group tool counts. The destination label is enough.

## Non-goals

- No deletion of tool implementations, saved records, or direct legacy routes.
- No billing, tax, contract, or record-model changes.
- No claim that the retained tools are server-backed beyond their existing
  behavior.

## Acceptance

- A field user sees the five daily tools before any utility catalog.
- Supporting utilities are grouped by intent: Plan, Track, and Site.
- The hub does not promote duplicate money, daily-report, tax, legal-template,
  or unwired-earnings surfaces.
- Existing saved recent and field-tool preferences gracefully discard a tool
  that is no longer exposed from the hub.
