# Packet 26 - Entry Experience

## Goal

Make the first screen explain RIVT while giving both new and returning people an
obvious path. Product preview must never make sign-in look secondary.

## Scope

- Keep the swipeable capability preview and explicit demo route.
- Make `Create free account` and `Log in` persistent peer actions on every
  intro slide.
- Keep sample exploration as a clearly labeled third path instead of presenting
  it as a skipped account action.

## Non-goals

- No auth-provider, password, invite, or onboarding-data behavior changes.
- No fabricated live activity; the demo remains clearly labeled sample data.
- No change to the signed-in onboarding flow.

## Acceptance

- A returning member can choose `Log in` without advancing or dismissing the
  product tour.
- A new member can choose `Create free account` from every intro slide.
- Sample exploration is visibly distinct from account creation and login.
